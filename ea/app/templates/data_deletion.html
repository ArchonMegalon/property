Data deletion

Request deletion of your PropertyQuarry data.

PropertyQuarry keeps account and property-search data only for the features you enable. You can ask us to delete account records, saved searches, connected-channel data, and generated property files tied to your account.

1. Send the request Email {{ contact_email }} with the subject line "Data deletion request" and the email address or phone number used for the account.
2. We verify ownership We may ask you to confirm the request from the same email, sign-in channel, or secure account link before deleting records.
3. We remove retained data After confirmation, we delete or anonymize account, search, channel-connection, and generated property records unless retention is legally required.
Scope

What the request covers

Deletion applies to data controlled by PropertyQuarry. Data held by connected providers must also be managed in those providers' own account settings.

  • Account identity records, sign-in metadata, saved search preferences, and saved review settings.
  • Connected-channel metadata and authorization records stored by PropertyQuarry.
  • Generated recommendations, shortlist records, tours, dossiers, and property review files tied to your account.
  • Backups and logs are removed or aged out according to operational retention windows unless legal obligations require longer retention.
