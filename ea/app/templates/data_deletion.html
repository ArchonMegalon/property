Retention and recovery

What remains after confirmation

A digest-only erasure tombstone prevents a rolling backup from restoring deleted data as an active account.

  • Primary account, search, shortlist, preference, packet, artifact, event, and delivery records are removed.
  • Sessions are revoked immediately; revoked tour routes fail closed while cache purge is queued.
  • Provider bindings are removed locally. Provider-side deletion remains pending until a provider receipt arrives.
  • Backups expire by the lifecycle deadline. A restore must reapply the tombstone before services start.
  • A legal hold must appear explicitly in the receipt; none is assumed by default.
