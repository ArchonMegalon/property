Why it exists

Property search is usually broad, repetitive, and unreasoned.

PropertyQuarry compresses the work into one flow: preference capture, cross-platform crawl, ranking, enrichment, hosted review, and feedback back into the profile.

Personalized search

Use saved preferences like heating type, family fit, transit access, and neighborhood posture as first-class ranking inputs.

Explainable research

The shortlisted property page shows what was verified, what is inferred, and what is still missing.

Hosted review surface

Links should land on the branded packet with the research, not on the vendor portal with no context.
