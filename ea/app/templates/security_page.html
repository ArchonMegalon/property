Security

See what PropertyQuarry can do before you let it act.

Research stays visible, permissions stay explicit, and every recommendation stays attached to the property. The goal is dependable buying research, not hidden automation.

Can PropertyQuarry send or change things without me? Research, ranking, and recommendations can be enabled early. Sensitive actions and outbound delivery can stay reviewable so the user remains in control.
Does it imply broader access than it really has? No. Each channel has a visible support contract so the product does not promise read, write, import, or sync behavior it cannot actually deliver.
Does useful context disappear after each session? No. The account can keep preferences, comments, property facts, and recurring fit signals so the search improves over time instead of starting from zero every day.
Reviewable actions Review gates and explicit follow-up actions keep the product safe in real property research work.
Explicit channel behavior Connection pages explain what is live, what is guided, and what still stays manual.
Durable account memory Context stays attached to the right account instead of floating around in isolated prompts.
