Product

One property research app for the search that usually gets rebuilt by hand.

PropertyQuarry is designed for the daily loop around search posture, shortlist ranking, research packets, 3D tour review, and buyer-specific fit signals. It is strongest when the search is real, recurring, and spread across too many portals.

A normal day

Start with the shortlist

  • See which listings actually match your profile.
  • See what stands out and what still needs checking.
  • See where the shortlist is weak or incomplete.
  • See which items deserve a deeper research run.
What it replaces

Less manual portal-hopping

  • Less reopening the same search filters on every portal.
  • Less manual comparison across listings that look similar but are not.
  • Less switching between maps, floor plans, galleries, and agent emails just to decide what is next.
  • Less shallow AI summary with no source trail or review point.
