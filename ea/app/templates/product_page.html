Product

One assistant workspace for the work that usually gets rebuilt by hand.

Executive Assistant is designed for the daily loop around a morning memo, one queue, commitments, people, and evidence. It is strongest when the work is real, recurring, and spread across too many threads and calendars.

A normal day

Start with the morning memo

  • See what changed overnight.
  • See who needs a reply before lunch.
  • See which promises or deadlines are starting to slip.
  • See which items need review before anything is sent.
What it replaces

Less manual reconstruction

  • Less re-reading old threads to remember context.
  • Less rebuilding of the same commitment list every morning.
  • Less switching between email, calendar, and chat just to decide what is next.
  • Less generic drafting with no source trail or review point.
