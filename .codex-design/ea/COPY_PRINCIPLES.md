# EA Copy Principles

## Write like this

- use concrete office language
- describe outcomes before system design
- say what happens next
- say what still needs approval
- keep trust language explicit and short

## Avoid this

- internal product-direction language like "should", "goal", or "product rule" on customer pages
- control-plane or deployment language on first-visit pages
- inherited Chummer, community, campaign, or organizer terminology
- abstract claims about intelligence without visible work

## Canonical phrases

- "Morning brief"
- "Queue"
- "Commitment"
- "Review before send"
- "Workspace access"
- "Evidence"
- "Rules"
