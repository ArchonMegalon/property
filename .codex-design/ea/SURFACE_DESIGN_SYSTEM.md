# EA Surface Design System

## Tone

Calm, direct, and office-grade.
The surface should feel like a dependable chief-of-staff system, not a playground, dashboard theme, or internal product memo.

## What the UI should foreground

- what changed
- what needs a decision
- what needs approval
- what is at risk of slipping
- what evidence supports the recommendation

## What the UI should bury

- implementation lore
- route or provider jargon
- legacy community language
- control-plane posture before first value

## Delivery-first rule

The strongest product moments happen outside the dashboard:

- 08:00 morning brief
- approval link
- draft review
- reminder before a commitment slips

The browser deepens trust and control; it should not be the only place where the product feels alive.
