# Executive Assistant Vision

## Product

Executive Assistant is the operating system for one executive office.
It turns Gmail and Calendar first into one morning memo, one decision queue, one commitment system, one draft-review lane, and a durable evidence-backed office memory.

## Primary objects

- `people`
- `threads`
- `commitments`
- `decisions`
- `drafts`
- `evidence`
- `rules`

## Default experience

The principal should mostly experience EA through delivered work:

- morning memo delivery
- inline approval links
- draft review
- commitment reminders
- handoff summaries

The browser is for deeper review:

- history and audit
- queue clearing
- commitment tracking
- people context
- evidence review
- rule editing
- operator collaboration

## Product boundary

Lead with:

- one executive
- one operator
- Gmail + Calendar first
- review before send
- durable office context

Defer until later:

- broad multi-channel sprawl
- complex admin posture on first visit
- automation that hides where approval happens
- product language inherited from Chummer, campaigns, or community operations
