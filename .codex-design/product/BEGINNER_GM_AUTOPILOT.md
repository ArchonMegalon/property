# Beginner GM autopilot

## Purpose

This file defines the lowest-friction “run your first table” path for a new GM.

## Canonical rule

Beginner GM autopilot is a guided setup and closeout lane.

It may automate checklist assembly, packet drafting, scheduling prompts, and resolution reminders.
It may not silently take campaign authority or outcome authority away from the GM.

## Wizard flow

1. Pick a beginner one-shot or starter job packet.
2. Pick the active community rule environment.
3. Pick quickstart runner packs.
4. Pick session length and table size.
5. Pick table contract and safety notes.
6. Pick scheduling mode.
7. Pick meeting handoff.
8. Generate opposition packet and prep checklist.
9. Publish the open run.
10. After play, file resolution and recap.

## Outputs

- one `OpenRun`
- one `TableContract`
- quickstart runner suggestions
- one opposition packet
- one scheduling receipt
- one post-session resolution prompt

## Why this matters

The product does not only need more players.
It needs more GMs who can survive their first run and want to do it again.
