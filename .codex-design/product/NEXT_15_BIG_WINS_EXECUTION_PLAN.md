# Chummer next 15 big wins execution plan

## Ordering rule

Close canon and validation first, then make the campaign middle real, then build the differentiation moat, then widen portability, publishing, and grounded help.

## Wave 0

1. Publish the closeout you already claim.
2. Replace snippet validation with schema and invariant validation.
3. Automate the weekly product pulse for real.

## Wave 1

4. Make `Chummer.Campaign.Contracts` executable.
5. Ship the living dossier as a real product object.
6. Deliver campaign continuity end to end.

## Wave 2

7. Canonize and build Build Lab.
8. Make Rules Navigator a flagship Explain Everywhere product.
9. Add interop as a first-class product promise.
10. Finish migration from legacy character-file thinking to portable dossier and campaign packages.

## Wave 3

11. Turn the public release surface into a real trust surface.
12. Build the grounded support assistant on top of real case truth.
13. Create the organizer and community operator layer.
14. Turn creator publication into a second pillar.
15. Refresh the public story around Build / Explain / Run / Publish / Improve.

## The 15 wins

### 1. Publish the closeout you already claim

Owners: `chummer6-design`, `fleet`, `chummer6-hub`, `chummer6-ui`, `chummer6-hub-registry`

Exit: roadmap, milestone registry, release evidence, README, and public progress wording all agree that the first front-door plus support plus governor wave is materially closed on public `main`.

### 2. Replace snippet validation with schema + invariant validation

Owners: `chummer6-design`, `fleet`

Exit: contracts, milestones, progress artifacts, release-shelf docs, and control-loop docs fail on schema or consistency drift instead of only on file existence or a few anchor strings.

### 3. Automate the weekly product pulse for real

Owners: `chummer6-design`, `fleet`, `chummer6-hub`

Exit: `PRODUCT_HEALTH_SCORECARD.yaml` is backed by a routine publication path that records weekly snapshots, feeds the public progress history, and gives the Product Governor bounded evidence for freeze or reroute decisions.

### 4. Make `Chummer.Campaign.Contracts` executable

Owners: `chummer6-hub`, `chummer6-ui`, `chummer6-mobile`, `chummer6-design`

Exit: runner dossier, crew, campaign, scene, objective, recap, and continuity projections are package-owned runtime truth rather than only design vocabulary.

### 5. Ship the living dossier as a real product object

Owners: `chummer6-hub`, `chummer6-ui`, `chummer6-mobile`

Exit: a runner can move from build receipt to living dossier, through play and return, without head-local identity drift or recap loss.

### 6. Deliver campaign continuity end to end

Owners: `chummer6-hub`, `chummer6-mobile`, `chummer6-core`, `chummer6-media-factory`

Exit: campaign continuity survives build, live play, reconnect, recap, and artifact publication with one replay-safe chain of truth.

### 7. Canonize and build Build Lab

Owners: `chummer6-design`, `chummer6-core`, `chummer6-ui`

Exit: Build Lab has explicit canon, deterministic backend seams, first-class UI, and living-dossier handoff rather than existing as a downstream milestone label.

### 8. Make Rules Navigator a flagship Explain Everywhere product

Owners: `chummer6-core`, `chummer6-ui`, `chummer6-design`

Exit: rule lookup, provenance, before versus after tradeoffs, and why-changed traces become a first-class public and signed-in experience rather than an internal debug affordance.

### 9. Add interop as a first-class product promise

Owners: `chummer6-core`, `chummer6-hub`, `chummer6-design`

Exit: import, export, package portability, and compatibility posture are named product promises instead of compatibility side quests.

### 10. Finish migration from legacy character-file thinking to portable dossier/campaign packages

Owners: `chummer6-core`, `chummer6-hub`, `chummer6-ui`, `chummer6-design`

Exit: the product story, runtime seams, and user workflows all center dossiers and campaign packages rather than old single-file character cargo.

### 11. Turn the public release surface into a real trust surface

Owners: `chummer6-hub`, `chummer6-hub-registry`, `chummer6-ui`, `fleet`

Exit: `/downloads`, release notes, known issues, support status, and update posture read like one honest trust surface tied to registry and support truth.

### 12. Build the grounded support assistant on top of real case truth

Owners: `chummer6-hub`, `chummer6-design`, `fleet`

Exit: a later assistant answers only from curated case, help, and release truth and hands off cleanly when confidence is low.

### 13. Create the organizer and community operator layer

Owners: `chummer6-hub`, `executive-assistant`, `chummer6-design`

Exit: groups, organizers, campaign operators, and community stewards get first-class operational surfaces without inventing a second account or control model.

### 14. Turn creator publication into a second pillar

Owners: `chummer6-media-factory`, `chummer6-hub`, `chummer6-design`

Exit: creator publication is no longer an implied side benefit of dossiers and recaps; it becomes a named product pillar with manifest, review, and distribution truth.

### 15. Refresh the public story around Build / Explain / Run / Publish / Improve

Owners: `chummer6-design`, `chummer6-hub`, `Chummer6`

Exit: landing, guide, progress, and help surfaces all tell one coherent story grounded in the executable middle of the product instead of drifting into repo-shaped descriptions.
