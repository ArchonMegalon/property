# Public media and guide asset policy

## Purpose

Public landing images, public guide images, narrated clips, recap assets, and similar generated media are downstream artifacts.
They are not canonical product meaning by themselves.

## Rules

* Generated landing and guide assets are downstream, non-canonical artifacts.
* A full public-surface generation pass may use one style epoch for coherence.
* Different public-surface generations may deliberately use different style epochs.
* Exact text in guide images must be post-composited or otherwise verified, not trusted to raw model lettering.
* Visual style may vary, but product meaning must stay subordinate to canonical design truth.
* Media receipts, provenance, and manifest ownership stay in Chummer-owned records, not vendor dashboards.
* Horizon art must follow `HORIZON_DESIGN_INSTRUCTIONS.md`: vivid product scenes, readable action, and AR text only when it fits what a runner, GM, fixer, streetdoc, rigger, decker, or organizer would actually see.
* Horizon art relevance is reviewed against `HORIZON_IMAGE_RELEVANCE_AUDIT.md`; a pretty image is not enough if it does not explain the horizon's product moment.

## Owner posture

* `chummer6-design` owns the policy.
* `chummer6-media-factory` owns media execution and receipts.
* `chummer6-hub` owns the hosted landing projection on `chummer.run`.
* `Chummer6` may publish the downstream outputs.
