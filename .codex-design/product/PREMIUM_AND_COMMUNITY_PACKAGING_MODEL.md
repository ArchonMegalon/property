# Premium and Community Packaging Model

## Purpose

This file defines the monetization and packaging guardrails for Chummer's campaign OS.

Premium may buy capacity, convenience, publishing, media, organizer tools, or human service lanes.
Premium must not buy faction victory, leaderboard rank, rules advantage, safety bypass, or support truth.

## Candidate packages

- free desktop builder
- claimed install plus cloud continuity
- GM workspace premium
- creator publication premium
- BLACK LEDGER private world
- Community Hub season tools
- Table Pulse debrief packs
- KARMA FORGE creator/community packs
- human GM clinics via Lunacal
- artifact generation quotas
- public creator/showcase pages

## Validation stack

Use:

- Vizologi for strategy exploration
- Icanpreneur for interviews
- MetaSurvey for quant validation
- ProductLift for public demand
- Lunacal for clinics and sales/service calls
- Emailit for closeout and lifecycle notices
- Teable for package-review workbench projection

## Hard boundaries

Premium must not:

- unlock better rules math
- buy faction or season advantage
- buy public leaderboard placement
- hide support or privacy controls
- bypass campaign consent
- make private user data part of marketing
- make Invoiless or any billing tool entitlement truth

Hub owns entitlement truth.
