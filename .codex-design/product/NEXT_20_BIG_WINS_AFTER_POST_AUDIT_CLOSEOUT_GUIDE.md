# Chummer next 20 big wins after post-audit closeout

## Precondition

Publish a real closeout for the current `POST_AUDIT_NEXT_20_BIG_WINS` wave in `chummer6-design`, update
`ROADMAP.md`, flip the active registry state, refresh the public guide bundle, and make the weekly pulse
reflect the new baseline.

## Framing

This guide starts after the post-audit wave is honestly closed in canon.

The job now is:

1. make the campaign OS indispensable,
2. widen the Build / Explain moat,
3. make exchange, output, and publication a second pillar,
4. turn trust, operator depth, and launch readiness into boring product truth.

The machine-readable control plane for this wave lives in
`NEXT_20_BIG_WINS_AFTER_POST_AUDIT_CLOSEOUT_REGISTRY.yaml`.

## Ordering rule

Close the post-audit closeout truth first.
Then make the campaign OS indispensable across Hub, UI, mobile, and Registry.
Then widen Build / Explain.
Then turn publication, trust, and launch scale into visible product strengths.

## Wave 1 - make the campaign OS indispensable

### 1. Campaign workspace v3: downtime, aftermath, and long-lived campaign memory
Owners: `chummer6-hub`, `chummer6-ui`, `chummer6-mobile`
Exit: campaign state covers session plan -> run -> recap -> downtime -> next session without lossy handoffs.

### 2. Faction, heat, contact, and reputation state as first-class campaign truth
Owners: `chummer6-hub`, `chummer6-core`, `chummer6-ui`
Exit: campaign consequences are durable objects, not recap prose or local notes.

### 3. Crew and roster operations across campaigns and groups
Owners: `chummer6-hub`, `executive-assistant`, `chummer6-ui`
Exit: organizers and GMs can move dossiers between rosters, campaigns, and groups with explicit ownership and auditability.

### 4. NPC/opposition packets and GM prep library
Owners: `chummer6-hub`, `chummer6-core`, `chummer6-media-factory`, `chummer6-ui`
Exit: reusable opposition, scene, and prep packets are governed, searchable, and campaign-bindable.

### 5. Safehouse / travel mode with proactive offline prefetch
Owners: `chummer6-hub`, `chummer6-hub-registry`, `chummer6-ui`, `chummer6-mobile`
Exit: a claimed device can prefetch the exact dossier, campaign, rule environment, and artifact set needed for travel and offline use.
