# Account-aware front door closeout

Last reviewed: 2026-03-25

## Purpose

Record the closeout of the Account-Aware Front Door wave so roadmap, milestone, and release-evidence language matches the public-main implementation instead of lagging it.

## Material closeout on public `main`

* `chummer6-design` now carries downloads, auto-update, release-shelf, install-linking, support, and governor canon with validator coverage.
* `chummer6-hub` now renders `/downloads`, `/home`, and `/account` from canonical landing and registry truth, including linked identities, channel-link posture, claimable installs, install guidance, and support-case history.
* `chummer6-hub-registry` now owns promoted desktop release heads, installer and update metadata, compatibility posture, and registry-backed release projections consumed by Hub and UI.
* `chummer6-ui` now ships one candidate across Windows, macOS, and Linux release targets, runs startup smoke before promotion, supports install claiming, and emits structured crash, bug, and feedback intake.
* `fleet` now consumes the same governor and support-loop canon, materializes support packets, and routes startup-smoke or release-regression evidence through the product-governor loop.
* `executive-assistant` now sits inside the mirrored control-plane perimeter as a governed synthesis substrate instead of an under-modeled sidecar.

## Language correction

* Foundation release complete at the canonical product and design level: true.
* Account-aware front door, desktop update, first support plane, and first product-governor loop materially closed on public `main`: true.
* Public product maturity still advancing in campaign-spine execution, living-dossier depth, weekly pulse history, Build Lab productization, and broader promotion: also true.

Those statements no longer conflict once "foundation complete" is kept separate from "all additive product waves are done."

## What remains additive

* weekly history depth must grow before public ETA can lean on measured velocity instead of mostly planning bands
* `Chummer.Campaign.Contracts` and the living dossier still need to become the executable middle of the product
* Build Lab, organizer or community operations, and creator publication depth remain additive growth lanes rather than closeout debt
