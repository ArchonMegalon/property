# Organize a community and close the loop

## Happy path

1. An organizer or community operator creates or joins a governed group, links the right identities or channels, and sees the current participation, release, and support posture in one place.
2. Community invites, sponsorship or boost posture, and install or support guidance all compile from the same Hub-owned account and ledger substrate.
3. When feedback, crash clusters, or release pain repeat, Fleet and the Product Governor route that evidence into code, docs, queue, or canon changes instead of leaving it as loose chatter.
4. The organizer can point members to the real downloads, known-issue, and closure surfaces without reverse-engineering repo status.

## Failure modes

* If a join code, boost code, or channel link is stale, the user-visible error must explain the real recovery path instead of leaking operator vocabulary.
* If a community issue repeats across members, raw reports must cluster before they become work or canon truth.
* If the public story or support posture drifts from real release truth, the Product Governor must be able to freeze, reroute, or escalate instead of letting the organizer carry that contradiction manually.
