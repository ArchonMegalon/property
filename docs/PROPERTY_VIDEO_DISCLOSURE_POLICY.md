# Property Video Disclosure Policy

PropertyQuarry videos and scripts must preserve the distinction between observed facts, source-bound claims, unknowns, and generated material.

## Required Labels

Use labels such as:

- Generated from the reviewed dossier.
- Facts observed on the stated date.
- Verify current facts at the source.
- Illustrative visualization.
- Synthetic tour preview.
- Actual listing image.

## Forbidden Language

Do not use:

- dream home;
- hidden gem;
- best deal;
- perfect investment;
- guaranteed return;
- safe investment;
- legally compliant;
- no hidden problems.

## Tour Narration

Tour narration may describe only visible or supplied facts. It must not invent room layout, condition, finishes, view, furniture, or renovation status.

Generated visualization is not a measured survey. Dimensions, furniture, and finishes may be inaccurate and must be checked against official documents and an in-person viewing.
