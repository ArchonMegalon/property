# Brand

## Name

PropertyQuarry

## Why the name works

- `Property` is the clearest English-language category word
- `Quarry` suggests digging out the worthwhile findings from a noisy landscape
- the name supports search, ranking, and research rather than just browsing
- it can expand from rentals to purchases without sounding too narrow

## Positioning line

Find properties that fit the person, not just the filter.

## Tone

- calm
- sharp
- practical
- evidence-driven
- not gimmicky

## Visual direction

- restrained product UI
- dark or neutral shell with warm data accents
- dense but readable listing comparison
- no marketing-style fluff

## Naming constraints

Avoid:

- `AI` suffixes in the public product name
- generic portal wording like `search`, `finder`, or `app` as the whole brand
- overpromising autonomy before the research layer is actually strong
