# Product Brief

## Product

PropertyQuarry

## Category

AI-assisted property discovery and research

## Problem

Property search is fragmented across portals and optimized for inventory browsing, not for personal fit.

Users still do manual work to answer questions like:

- Which listings actually fit my life?
- Which ones are missing critical information?
- Which options deserve a real viewing?
- Which details matter for this specific buyer or renter?

## Core value

PropertyQuarry aggregates listings, ranks them against personal preferences, and produces research-backed property pages that help a user decide where to spend attention.

## Initial audience

### Primary

- renters in urban markets
- buyers who want help screening many listings quickly

### Secondary

- relocation-heavy professionals
- family households with specific constraints
- advisors or scouts helping another decision-maker

## Product pillars

1. Discovery
   - search major portals
   - support multiple countries and research languages
   - switch provider sets by market
   - keep budget posture market-aware instead of assuming one currency everywhere
   - save profile preferences
   - re-run searches over time

2. Ranking
   - score listings against personal fit
   - explain why a match stands out
   - support negative feedback and preference learning

3. Research
   - enrich listing gaps
   - estimate nearby amenities
   - investigate heating, lift, transit, and family-relevant details

4. Review
   - hosted property packet
   - source links
   - structured findings
   - tour surface when available

## Anti-goals

- not a generic MLS clone
- not just another portal wrapper
- not an LLM chat toy with no real retrieval
- not a luxury marketing site

## Monetization stance

Freemium entry with paid research depth.

## Current flagship scope

- Austria, Germany, Switzerland, United Kingdom, Spain, Italy, France, Netherlands, and United States market selection
- per-country provider catalog in the account
- language-aware property brief defaults
- provider-specific source generation as the crawl entry layer
