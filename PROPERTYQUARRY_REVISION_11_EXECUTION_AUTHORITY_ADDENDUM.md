## Authority split

PropertyQuarry retains final authority over source truth, rooms, portals,
walkable geometry, exact route, style selection, asset rights, user consent,
purpose, privacy policy, retention, deletion, takedown, product projection,
publication, restoration, user closeout, and readiness language.

The exact PropertyQuarry consumer-authorization owner is repository
`/docker/property`, package `app.product`, module
`app.product.property_tour_hosting`. Its stable evidence owner token is
`propertyquarry.app.product.property_tour_hosting`. It alone owns consumer
authorization issuance, revocation, and product-side execution freeze.

The exact privacy and retention owner remains repository `/docker/property`,
package `app.api.routes`, module `app.api.routes.landing`. It owns privacy and
retention tombstone requests, deletion requests, and the stop-new-execution
decision. It does not own quota history or provider-attempt history. The
product bridge may validate and carry a privacy-policy receipt but cannot amend
policy, restore deleted material, or close a privacy case.

The shared governed media-factory lane, under the stable operation identity
`chummer6-media-factory`, exclusively owns provider-job submission and
cancellation, private provider receipts, attempt state, quota reserve,
consume, retry, cancel, release, and compensation journals, provider kill
switches, encrypted execution material, deterministic rendering, and
independent artifact verification. The EA host executes those operations only
under that media-factory identity; hosting does not make EA an operation owner.
Processing custody does not confer PropertyQuarry truth, authorization,
privacy, product, publication, or readiness authority.

Fleet owns budget ceilings, canary start and stop, rollback, and landing. The
PropertyQuarry product governor may freeze or reroute product demand but cannot
mutate provider attempts, quota history, or privacy history. No other actor may
claim any of these operation-owner roles by configuration or prose delegation.

EA remains non-authoritative for PropertyQuarry product meaning. It may host the
shared implementation and derived redacted telemetry but may not fabricate
consumer authorization, numeric policy evidence, rights, consent, provider
capability, quota, output quality, publication, or readiness.

private execution-material store.

- A durable delete tombstone is recorded before ciphertext unlink.
